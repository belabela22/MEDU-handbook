SWM | Medical Education’s Handbook

By Luna.C.Everly – Medical Education Overseer

🩺 Introduction to MEDU

Welcome to the SWM Medical Education Department (MEDU). As a vital part of the SWM Network, our department is committed to educating, training, and developing future healthcare leaders. We believe in excellence, empathy, and innovation in everything we teach.

This handbook serves as your comprehensive guide, outlining responsibilities, expectations, training curriculum, and advancement pathways.

⚕️ Department Rules & Requirements

Key Requirements:
  • Maintain at least 70% attendance in all MEDU sessions.
  • Actively participate in theoretical and practical modules.
  • Communicate respectfully and follow the chain of command.

📋 Communication Guidelines

Effective and respectful communication is expected at all times. Use appropriate channels (e.g., #medu-questions, DMs for mentor contact) and avoid spamming or off-topic discussions during lectures or evaluations.

🧠 Training Curriculum

The curriculum is divided into:

  • Foundational Modules: Anatomy, Medical Ethics, Patient Interaction.
  • Clinical Modules: Emergency Response, Triage, Diagnostics.
  • Advanced Simulations: Real-time scenarios, ICU simulations, Field Med Evacs.
📌 Tip: Always keep notes from live trainings—they may appear in your evaluations.

📅 Promotion System

Promotions are based on performance, evaluation scores, leadership, and contribution. To move up:

  • Maintain consistent attendance.
  • Pass all evaluations with at least 80% accuracy.
  • Demonstrate initiative during simulations.

🎓 Graduation & Certification

Graduates who complete all core modules and pass final assessments will be awarded a MEDU Certificate. This grants eligibility for leadership positions and mentorship roles.

⚠️ Strike & Suspension Policy

⚠️ 3-Strikes Rule:
  • 1st Strike: Warning issued.
  • 2nd Strike: Temporary suspension & retraining.
  • 3rd Strike: Expulsion from the MEDU program.

👩‍🏫 Mentorship Program

Our mentorship initiative pairs new trainees with seasoned MEDU graduates. This one-on-one system allows for tailored learning and guidance throughout your journey.

🗂️ Document Handling

Members are expected to manage documentation professionally. This includes proper formatting, citation, and timely submission of required forms and reports.

📢 Event Participation

Attend special events like medical drills, Q&A panels, and guest lectures. Your attendance boosts your visibility and networking within the SWM Network.

💼 Medical Education Internship

Internships are offered to top-performing members. These roles involve co-hosting sessions, preparing materials, and evaluating peers under supervision.
