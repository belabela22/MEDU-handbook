🩺 Section 1: Introduction to MEDU

Welcome to the Medical Education Department (MEDU) of the SWM Network. Our mission is to train, support, and elevate future medical leaders within the community.

⚕️ Section 2: Department Rules & Requirements

Reminder: Members must adhere to all SWM general rules and maintain professionalism at all times.
  • Be active during sessions and evaluations.
  • Show respect toward superiors, trainees, and colleagues.
  • Report any misconduct to the MEDU Overseer or higher authorities.

📋 Section 3: Communication Guidelines

Use clear and respectful communication. Avoid spamming, and always maintain a professional tone in all MEDU-related chats and documents.

🧠 Section 4: Training Curriculum

The curriculum includes theoretical sessions, practical simulations, and peer discussions. Members are expected to attend at least 75% of the sessions.

📅 Section 5: Promotion System

Promotions within MEDU are based on merit, attendance, test scores, and observed professionalism.

🎓 Section 6: Graduation & Certification

Upon successful completion of the program, members will receive a MEDU Certificate and will be eligible for MEDU Graduate roles.

⚠️ Section 7: Strike & Suspension Policies

⚠️ Warning: Accumulating 3 strikes will result in removal from the department.
  • 1st Strike: Verbal warning and record log.
  • 2nd Strike: Temporary suspension and retraining.
  • 3rd Strike: Removal and report to SWM Command.

👩‍🏫 Section 8: Mentorship Program

MEDU offers a one-on-one mentorship system to help trainees grow and learn in a safe and supportive environment.

🗂️ Section 9: Document Handling

Ensure all educational material is uploaded on time and follows formatting standards. Use Google Docs or SWM-approved platforms.

📢 Section 10: Event Participation

All MEDU members are encouraged to join educational events, workshops, and simulations hosted within or outside SWM.

💼 Section 11: Medical Education Internship

Internships are available for advanced trainees. Requirements include: at least 2 months of participation and recommendation from a mentor.
